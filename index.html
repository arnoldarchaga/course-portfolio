My Top 5 Favorite Movies

  1. Interstellar

    I like this movie because it mixes space, science, and emotional storytelling in a really powerful way.

  2. The Dark Knight

    This is one of my favorite superhero movies because of the action, serious tone, and memorable characters.

  3. Creed

    I enjoy this movie because it is motivational and shows the importance of discipline, confidence, and hard work.

  4. Fast Five

    This movie is fun to watch because it has cars, action, and a good team-based story.

  5. Spider-Man: No Way Home

    I like this movie because it brings different Spider-Man stories together and has a lot of nostalgic moments.
